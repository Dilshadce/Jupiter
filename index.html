Quality is the highest Priority

Jupiter Advanced Projects established in the year 2004 under the banner of Jupiter International Trading later Rebranded as Jupiter Advanced Projects (JAP).

JAP adopted its main policy as “Quality is the highest Priority”, which was the main reason that helps the Company grow to fame, stability in a very professional manner. The company is specialized mainly in Steel structural Fabrication works, with specialized division to ensure craftsmanship and quality of the highest order.
