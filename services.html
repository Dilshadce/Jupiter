Our Services

We Build Your Dreams

J.A.P offers structural steel detailing, design, fabrication and erection to the clients across the G.C.C & Arab Countries. Our in -house design team works together with detailers to develop detailed plans, sketches for all types of steel structural works.

J.A.P specializes in designing safe steel structures without compromising on aesthetics and over all functionality. our workshop at Barka Industrial Estate is equipped with all machinery required for structural steel fabrication. Work shop machine layout has been planned to assure smooth flow of the production and easy handling of materials. Our in-house Installation team is experience personals and they can handle any type of Installation works in Oman or MENA region.

For the service, J.A.P commits to offer all aspects structural steel, design, manufacturing and Installation. At its aim to meet the client ‘s needs and bring improvement to the organization such as follows.
